Problem Statement

Why Do We Need AI-Powered Fault Detection?

⚠️
Telecom networks generate millions of data points daily — manual monitoring is impossible at scale
Network operators are overwhelmed with data volume
📉
Network faults cause service outages, revenue loss, and customer dissatisfaction
Average outage costs $5,600+ per minute for enterprises
⏳
Traditional reactive approaches detect faults only AFTER they occur
By then, damage is already done — too late to prevent
🔍
No intelligent system to predict and prevent failures before they happen
Existing tools lack predictive capabilities
🎯

Our Goal

Build an AI-powered system that predicts network faults BEFORE they occur, enabling proactive maintenance, reducing downtime, and improving service quality.
